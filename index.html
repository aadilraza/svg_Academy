SERVICES
WE COVER IT ALL! In addition to polishing their craft, aspiring performers should learn to be strong entrepreneurs.
THIS IS WHERE WE COME IN: This immersive program aids in: 1. Cultivating a sustainable business acting (goal) plan. 2. Understanding of the film/tv acting industry from the executive's angle. 3. Creating competitive tools, an online presence and a productive structure for a strong launch towards professional work. 4. Meeting new performing friends and industry professionals.
Just like with any other start-up venture, in order to be successful, performers will write plan for goal and business clarity.
We’ll learn to audition, network and study smarter by learning the industry trends on a global level. Which cities in the world have thriving film industries? How to find productions heading to your closest states? What do upcoming submission trends look like and how to be prepared!
Having an active social media presence is REQUIREDfor performers. As in any business it gives a sense of what the company (person) is about and executives review social media all the time. We will help launch or develop the sites necessary for a bright online presence.
We will cover all types of important networking and how to utilize opportunities and business cards. From friends met in acting class to keeping logs of executives at a party, this is important for every age and every level of actor
Every participant receives a completed website with design elements of their choice. Optionally, we will update the performers current site.
Marketing tools for actors are something that is constantly evolving and is often not consistent across cities and stage to screen. We will have production units and top photographers set up to so participants walk away with the most up-to-date and competitive marketing tools.
